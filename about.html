About Us

AUTO REPAIR & BODY SERVICES IN WHITE PLAINS, NY

Total Care Auto Repair Inc., has been in the automotive industry for more than 24 years and it has been providing outstanding full service auto repair and body shop. Conveniently located in White Plains, NY. We focus on our customers, and to make sure that they are satisfied with the services we provide.


We are in business to help you maintain your vehicles and to keep them on the road. By having your car routinely maintained, its life span is enhanced and surprised breakdowns can be prevented. We use the latest technology to assess the problem and offer you alternatives to fix them. We guarantee all of our work and know you’ll be happy with the work done. Total Care Service Station Inc., your car’s best friend! ¡El mejor amigo de su auto!
